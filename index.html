Here's a time line of Sergei Prokofiev's life:

  • 1891 - Born in Sontsovka (now Sontsivka, Pokrovsk Raion, Donetsk Oblast, Ukraine)
  • 1903 - Enters the St. Petersburg Conservatory
  • 1908 - For the first time, Prokofiev performed with his works
  • 1909 - Prokofiev graduated from the Conservatory as a composer
  • 1914 - Prokofiev graduated from the Conservatory as a pianist
  • 1917 - Prokofiev graduated from the Conservatory as organist. During this period, Sergei Sergeevich created the operas "Maddalena", "The Gambler"
  • 1918 - After graduating from the conservatory, since 1918 Sergei Sergeevich toured a lot, visited Japan, the USA, London Paris
  • 1927 - Creates the opera "Fiery Angel"
  • 1932 - Records his Third Concert in London
  • 1936 - Sergei Sergeevich moves to Moscow, begins teaching at the Conservatory
  • 1938 - Completes work on the ballet "Romeo and Juliet"
  • 1941-1945 - Creates the ballet "Cinderella", the opera "War and Peace", music for the films "Ivan the Terrible" and "Alexander Nevsky"
  • 1948 - Completes work on the opera "The Story of a Real Man"
  • 1949 - The composer created the ballet "The Tale of the Stone Flower", the symphony-concert "Guarding the World"
  • 1953 - The great musician died of a hypertensive crisis
